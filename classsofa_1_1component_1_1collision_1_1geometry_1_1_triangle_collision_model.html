template<class TDataTypes>
class sofa::component::collision::geometry::TriangleCollisionModel< TDataTypes >

This class will create collision elements based on a triangle and/or quad mesh. It uses directly the information of the topology and the dof to compute the triangle normals, BB and BoundingTree. The class
