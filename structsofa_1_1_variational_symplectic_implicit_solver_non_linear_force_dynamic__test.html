Detailed Description

template<typename _DataTypes>
struct sofa::VariationalSymplecticImplicitSolverNonLinearForceDynamic_test< _DataTypes >

Dynamic solver test. Test the dynamic behavior of solver with non linear force : study a sun-planet system with gravitational force. Create a simulation with 2 mechanical objects: one MO fixed (the sun) and one MO (the planet) with a initial velocity perpendicular to the initial sun-planet axis. The time step h is computed with the period T of the sun-planet system: h = T/nbIter. From simulated positions (simu_x;simu_y) compute the angle of the ellipse angle = atan(simu_y/simu_x) then compute the radius radius = (1-e)*r0/(1-e*cos(angle)) with r0= initialRadius and e = eccentricity = ABS(r0*v0*v0/(g*M)-1) (v0 initial velocity) and finally the expected trajectory expected_x = radius*cos(angle) expected-y = radius*sin(angle) Check if simulated positions are on the right trajectory, that is to say compare simulated and expected positions. Check if positions are correct by comparing the expected planet position to the simulated position at period T. Check if Hamiltonian energy is constant during simulation: variational solver is used with incremental potential energy.
