Detailed Description

template<typename _DataTypes>
struct sofa::RungeKutta4ExplicitSolverDynamic_test< _DataTypes >

Dynamic solver test. Test the dynamic behavior of solver: study a mass-spring system under gravity initialize with spring rest length it will oscillate around its equilibrium position if there is no damping. The movement follows the equation: x(t)= A cos(wt + phi) with w the pulsation w=sqrt(K/M), K the stiffness, M the mass and phi the phase. In this test: x(t=0)= 1 and v(t=0)=0 and K = spring stiffness and phi = 0 of material thus x(t)= cos(wt) This tests generates the discrete mass position obtained with rungeKutta4 solver with different parameter values (K,M,h). Then it compares the effective mass position to the computed mass position every time step.
