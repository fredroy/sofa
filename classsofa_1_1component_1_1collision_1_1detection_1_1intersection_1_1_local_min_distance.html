Detailed Description

Intersection methods using proximities. Filters are added to limit the number of contacts. The following pairs of collision models are supported:

  • Cube/Cube
  • Sphere/Sphere
  • Sphere/Point
  • Point/Point
  • Line/Line
  • Line/Point
  • Line/Sphere
  • Triangle/Point
  • Triangle/Sphere
  • Ray/Triangle
  • Ray/Sphere The following pairs of collision models are ignored:
  • Triangle/Line
  • Triangle/Triangle
  • Ray/Point
  • Ray/Line
